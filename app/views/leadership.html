Advisory Board: Providing Guidance and Oversight

The Advisory Board plays a crucial role in providing guidance and oversight to Maseno University Christian Union. Comprised of experienced leaders and experts, the board ensures that the Christian Union stays true to its mission and values, and provides strategic direction for the organization. Through regular meetings and consultations, the Advisory Board offers valuable insights and recommendations to help the Christian Union thrive and fulfill its purpose.

Committees Serving Under the Executive Committee

The Maseno University Christian Union is supported by various committees that serve under the Executive Committee. These committees play a crucial role in the smooth functioning and growth of the Christian Union.

Discover How Leaders are Chosen to Guide the Christian Union

The Christian Union has a rigorous selection process to identify dedicated individuals who will lead and serve the community with integrity and passion.

Leadership Team: Planning and Executing Together

The leadership team of Maseno University Christian Union collaborates closely to plan and execute the Union's activities and events. They work together to ensure that all initiatives align with the Union's mission and goals. Through effective communication and coordination, the team ensures that each member's strengths and expertise are utilized to their fullest potential.

Strategic Planning

The leadership team engages in strategic planning to set clear objectives and timelines for each initiative.

Task Delegation

Tasks and responsibilities are delegated among team members based on their skills and areas of expertise.

Effective Communication

The leadership team maintains open and transparent communication channels to ensure everyone is informed and aligned.
